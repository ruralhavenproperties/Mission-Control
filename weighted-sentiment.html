📊 Methodology: How We Weight Sources

1. Historical Accuracy Score

Track each source's predictions vs actual price movements over 12 months.

  • High: 80%+ accuracy → Weight 0.30-0.45
  • Medium: 60-79% accuracy → Weight 0.15-0.30
  • Low: <60% accuracy → Weight 0.05-0.15

2. Bias Adjustment

Producer sources weighted higher for supply news, consumer for demand.

  • Producer Bias: Better for supply disruptions, harvest data
  • Consumer Bias: Better for demand trends, consumption
  • Neutral: General market coverage

3. Geographic Relevance

Local sources weighted higher for regional commodities.

  • Vietnamese media → Coffee weight ×1.5
  • Saudi media → Oil weight ×1.4
  • Ghanaian media → Cocoa weight ×1.6
