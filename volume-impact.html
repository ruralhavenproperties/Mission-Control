📊 Market Context Adjustment Factors

1. Volume Environment

Adjust weights based on overall market volume:

  • High Volume Day: Media impact ×1.5
  • Normal Volume: Media impact ×1.0
  • Low Volume Day: Media impact ×0.7

2. Time of Day

Asian vs European vs US session impact:

  • Asian Session: Asian sources ×1.4
  • European Session: European sources ×1.3
  • US Session: All sources ×1.0

3. Market Regime

Trending vs ranging markets:

  • Strong Trend: Confirming news ×1.6
  • Ranging Market: All news ×1.0
  • Reversal: Contrarian news ×1.8

🎯 Final Impact Formula:

Adjusted Weight = Base Weight × Volume Multiplier × Market Context × Time Adjustment

Example: Arab News OPEC leak on high volume day during Asian session:
0.45 × 6.2 × 1.5 × 1.4 = 5.86 adjusted impact score
